White Russian

Description

The Big Lebowski was onto something with this creamy coffee-flavored cocktail.

A healthy dose of vodka in the mix assures that 'the dude abides.'

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 fluid ounces vodka
  • 1 fluid ounce coffee-flavored liqueur
  • 1 fluid ounce heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Fill an old-fashioned glass with ice.
  2. Pour in vodka and coffee liqueur.
  3. Top off with cream.
